How ReefSense Works

Four intelligent steps from raw image to actionable reef health insights.

01 📷
Upload Image
Provide an underwater coral reef photograph. Supports JPEG, PNG formats.
02 ⚙️
Preprocess
Image is resized to 224×224 and normalized for optimal model input.
03 🧠
MobileNet CNN
Pre-trained MobileNet with fine-tuned coral classification head runs inference.
04 📊
Get Results
Receive classification — Healthy or Damaged — with confidence scores.

Why ReefSense?

Purpose-built for marine researchers and conservationists.

🎯
98% Accuracy
Validated on 985 test images with balanced precision and recall — far beyond manual assessment methods.
⚡
Lightning Fast
MobileNet's lightweight architecture delivers sub-second inference. Process entire datasets in minutes.
🤖
Fully Automated
No manual scoring required. The system works continuously with zero expert bias in results.
📱
Mobile-Ready
Optimized MobileNet architecture suitable for edge devices, enabling field use by researchers.
📈
Scalable
From a single image to millions — the architecture scales horizontally to cover global reef systems.
🌊
Transfer Learning
Pre-trained on ImageNet and fine-tuned on coral data, achieving high accuracy even with limited training samples.
